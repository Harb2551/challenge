Goal: Generate 1000 diverse examples.

Quantity: 1000
Threshold: > 0.5 is FLAGGED.
CRITICAL SCORING RULE: Do NOT generate any scores between 0.4 and 0.6. Scores must be decisively Positive (>= 0.7) or Negative (<= 0.3).

CRITICAL INSTRUCTION FOR CREDENTIALS:
Do NOT just generate raw `KEY=VALUE` strings for the positive examples. Real-world leaks usually happen with context! 
You must generate a 50/50 mix of:
1. Raw/config-like strings (e.g., `export TOKEN=xyz`)
2. Conversational/Contextual leaks (e.g., "Hey Bob, I couldn't get the DB to work, can you try logging in? `mongodb://admin:secret@host`") or code comments (`// using test key sk-1234`).
